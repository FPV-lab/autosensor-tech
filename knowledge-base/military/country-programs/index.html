Key Conflicts

🇺🇦🇷🇺 Ukraine (2022-Present)

The defining drone war. Drones account for 60-70% of vehicle kills. Both sides use commercial and military drones extensively. Electronic warfare is a major factor.

🇦🇿 Nagorno-Karabakh (2020)

Azerbaijan used Turkish TB2 and Israeli Harop to devastating effect, destroying Armenian air defense and armor. A wake-up call for conventional militaries.

🇾🇪 Houthi Attacks (2019-Present)

Houthi forces use Iranian-supplied drones against Saudi oil infrastructure and Red Sea shipping. Demonstrates how cheap drones can threaten global trade.

Technology Trends (2025-2030)

  • AI Autonomy: Drones that identify and engage targets independently
  • Fiber-Optic Guidance: Wire-guided drones immune to jamming
  • Swarm Intelligence: Hundreds of drones acting autonomously
  • Counter-Countermeasures: AI-driven EW resistance
  • Directed Energy: Lasers and HPM for drone defense

Ethical Considerations

  • Autonomous weapons: Should machines decide who lives and dies?
  • Proliferation: Easy to manufacture, hard to control
  • International law: Existing treaties struggle to address drones
  • Dual-use: Agriculture tech can be weaponized
