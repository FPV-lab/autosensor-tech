⚙️ FPV Drone Components

Detailed guide to every component in an FPV drone

Frame - The Skeleton

Carbon fiber structure that holds all components. Available in True X (freestyle), Wide X (cinematic), and Stretched X (racing) layouts.

Flight Controller - The Brain

Circuit board with gyro/accelerometer that stabilizes flight. Runs Betaflight firmware at 8kHz loop time.

ProcessorSpeedMemoryStatus

ESC - Motor Controllers

Electronic Speed Controllers convert DC battery power to 3-phase AC for brushless motors. Usually bought as 4-in-1 boards.

Motors - The Power

Brushless motors spin the propellers. Size format: StatorWidth × StatorHeight (e.g., 2207 = 22mm × 7mm)

KV = RPM per Volt. Higher KV = faster but less torque. 1700-1900KV for 6S, 2400-2600KV for 4S.

Blades that convert motor rotation into lift. Format: Diameter × Pitch × Blades (e.g., 5×4.3×3)
