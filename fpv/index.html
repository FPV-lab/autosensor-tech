What is FPV?

FPV (First Person View) is a method of piloting a drone where the operator sees exactly what the drone's onboard camera sees in real-time, through specialized video goggles. This immersive perspective creates the sensation of sitting in the cockpit, enabling incredibly precise and dynamic control.


Unlike traditional camera drones which are GPS-stabilized, FPV drones are fully manual - the pilot has complete control over every movement. This enables:

  • Speeds exceeding 200 km/h
  • Acrobatic maneuvers: flips, rolls, powerloops
  • Precision flying through tight gaps
  • Cinematic shots impossible with any other camera system
