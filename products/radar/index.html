📡 Radar Detection System

Advanced pulse Doppler radar for all-weather drone detection and tracking

Key Advantage: Works on autonomous drones (no RF signal needed), all-weather 24/7 operation, multi-target tracking.

Overview

Our radar detection system uses advanced pulse Doppler technology to detect small drones at distances up to 10km. Unlike RF-based detection, radar works on any flying object regardless of whether it's transmitting. AI-enhanced classification distinguishes drones from birds and other clutter.
